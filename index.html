Welcome to Milk Rider!

Here's how to play:

  1. HOLD ANYWHERE to pedal (accelerate).
  2. MOVE CURSOR/FINGER up/down to steer smoothly within the road.
  3. Your milk trailer now fills based on your speed. Go fast to fill it fast!
  4. When the trailer is full, the floating Bottle Milk button glows. Tap it!
  5. Sell full trailers at houses by tapping the $ icon.
  6. Hit ramps to jump and collect rare milk bottles!
  7. Use the on-screen buttons in the top-left to upgrade on the fly!
  8. Check the Shop for powerful items!
